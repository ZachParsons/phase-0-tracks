Svalbard, Norway

Svalbard is a archipelago in the Arctic Ocean belonging to Norway. It is home to the world's northern-most town in the world, Longyearbyen, which is 817mi from the North Pole & has a population of 2,144. Yet due to the oceanic currents & jet stream the group of islands have a significantly warmer climate & prevalence of vegetation than other areas near that latitude. Longyearbyen's average high temperature in July is 44°F & its average low in January is -4°F.
